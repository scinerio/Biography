About Me

Hello! My name is Jason Williams, and I'm an early profession in the Information Security field that wants to be a "big shot" in the field one day. My passion for the field spawned from my first infosec job at TraceSecurity. Because of TraceSecurity, I self-studied to become a Security+ CE right before I graduated. Just less than a year after graduating, I decided to step my game up and take the CISSP exam, becoming an Associate of (ISC)2 until I meet the experience requirements. My next step is to become more well-versed in Offensive Security-- so I'm diving into as many machines as I can on hackthebox.eu until I feel that I'm ready for the OSCP. Having an offensive mindset is, in my opinion, one of the most valuable traits one could have in the field-- so I look forward to wearing a white hat soon.

Junior Cyber Defense Analyst

This position involved the use of numerous network monitoring tools to review and respond to various alerts for our clients. A usual day at DPS could involve detecting and responding to DoS/DDoS attacks, determining if an endpoint has malware, or investigating ongoing phishing campaigns against our customers.
